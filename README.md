Overview

GFM-Kable (Girlfriend Frequency Maximizer - Kable Edition) is the world's first open-source Relationship Foundation Model trained on:

- 0 successful relationships
- 12,487 ignored messages
- 3 years of Instagram research
- 847 "Seen" events
- 1.2 million friendship samples

Unlike ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and other expensive proprietary systems, GFM-Kable can generate realistic relationship simulations entirely offline.

Results may not transfer to production environments.

Why GFM-Kable?

Yet none of them can answer:

"Bro, how do I get a girlfriend?"

To address this critical research gap, we introduce GFM-Kable.
